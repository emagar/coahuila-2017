\frame {                      % SLIDE
\frametitle{The 2014 reform}

Surprising removal of the consecutive reelection ban\\ in effect since 1934

\begin{itemize}
\item<1> Members of Congress can reelect for 12 consecutive years
\item<1> ``Party lock'': incumbent must be re-nominated by same party
\item<1> Kick-off: 2021 mid-term
\end{itemize}

\bigskip \pause

  Reformers gave states institutional discretion
  \begin{itemize}
  \item<2> For state lawmakers: 2-, 3-, or 4-term limits
  \item<2> For municipal officers: single- or 2-term limits
  \item<2> Party lock mandatory
  \end{itemize}
  \bigskip
  Variable election calendars $\rightarrow$ incumbents on the ballot \alert{progressively}
